About Me

I'm Saumya, a final-year BCA student at Mega National College, Tribhuvan University in Lalitpur, Nepal. I like building things that work with clean interfaces, solid backends, and the plumbing that connects them.

My go-to stack is React on the frontend paired with either PHP + MySQL for traditional backends or Supabase when I want something modern and fast to ship. I've also worked with Java, C, and I'm currently picking up Next.js and Tailwind CSS to round out the full-stack picture.

I've deployed on Vercel, integrated payment gateways like eSewa, and built real-time features for actual users. I participated in the NCA International Conference and spend my free time exploring AI, cloud computing, and whatever problem lands in front of me. Open to frontend or full-stack roles and internships.
